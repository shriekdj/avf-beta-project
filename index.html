    Who are we

    We are here to change lives through VR tech innovation and uplift the various sectors of society.

    Ananda Vriddhi Foundation is a visionary non-profit organization dedicated to empowering various sectors of society by leveraging advanced technologies such as Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Mixed Reality (MR).

    Headquartered in Pune, our foundation utilizes the full capacity of Information Communication Technology to enhance educational opportunities for the underprivileged, elevate the mental well-being of the elderly, ensure pain management for critical patients to aid in rapid recovery, and revolutionize rural development by disseminating knowledge of global advancements through virtual experiences.

    Ananda Vriddhi Foundation works to empower different sectors of the society. Our primary aim is to spread happiness to the communities through dynamic strategies inspiring transformation.

    Know About Us

    We are an NGO redefining your contribution to society!

    Anand Vriddhi Foundation operates on a simple yet intense principle of spreading happiness and transforming lives by leveraging the latest technologies, such as Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Mixed Reality (MR). Our primary focus is empowering society, and ensuring the community&s well-being by devising innovative programs through VR. We are proud to be the first non-profit organization in India aiming to make tech accessible to all. Our team of experts works relentlessly to utilize digitization to spread smiles and community upliftment.

    Our Vision

    The elementary objective of the Ananda Vriddhi Foundation is to revolutionize and elevate lives by incorporating cutting-edge technology in education, cognitive well-being, health management, and experiential learning, facilitating a more inclusive and acquainted society that can benefit from the digital revolution.

    Our Mission

    Our motto is to deploy innovative technologies to enhance the educational experiences of underprivileged children, impart a positive spirit of happiness amongst the elderly by taking them to thoughtfully crafted virtual tours, provide immersive VR programs for the ailing patients helping in chronic pain management, and impart substantial virtual skill development experiences to a wide range of societal groups.

    Our Journey

    Why we started.

    Our journey began with realizing the need to spread happiness, motivation, and positivity for the upliftment of society, especially for underserved sectors of society. With a strong desire to contribute our best, we started the Anand Vriddhi Foundation and leveraged our technical expertise to bridge the digital gap and create new opportunities. By implementing advanced technologies, we help address the technological upgradation needed by the community and aspire to form a joyful and sustainable society.
